**Output**: Signal Shift (60.4, FAIL), Sequence Lock (66.5, SHIPPED via Top-3)

- Sequence Lock shipped despite below-threshold score (66.5) — Devil gave it 88 (strongest mechanical design)

### What Went Poorly
- Sequence Lock scored 38 on Ludus (mechanical depth) and 39 on Scout (originality)

### Lessons Learned

- When trendsetter ideas score low on depth, architect must compensate with enrichment layers
