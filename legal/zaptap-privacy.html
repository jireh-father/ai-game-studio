ZAP TAP — Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-05-26 · Last updated: 2026-05-26

This Privacy Policy explains how the mobile game ZAP TAP ("the App", package com.ruelstudio.sequencelock) collects, uses, and shares information. By using the App you agree to the terms below.

1. No account, no sign-up

The App requires no registration, login, email, or personal profile. Your game progress (cleared stages, best times, lives) is stored only on your device using local storage. We do not operate any game server, leaderboard, or cloud sync, and we do not collect your name, email, phone number, precise (GPS) location, contacts, photos, or files.

2. Information collected by third-party SDKs

The App itself stores data only on your device. However, the following third-party services integrated for ads and analytics may automatically collect limited information:

  • Google AdMob (advertising): may access the Android Advertising ID (AAID) and device/technical information (device model, OS version, coarse/approximate location inferred from IP) to serve and measure ads. You can reset the Advertising ID or opt out of personalized ads in your device settings.
  • Google Analytics for Firebase / Google Analytics (GA4) (analytics): collects an anonymous, app-generated client identifier plus aggregate usage events (e.g. stage start/clear, session start), device model, OS version, and approximate region, to help us understand gameplay and improve the App. This is not linked to your real identity.

3. How information is used

  • Display ads (including rewarded ads that grant in-game lives) via Google AdMob.
  • Understand aggregate gameplay and diagnose problems to improve the App.

We do not sell your personal information.

4. Third-party services

The following Google services are used, and their privacy policies also apply:

  • Google AdMob — https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads
  • Google Analytics — https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google Play Services — https://policies.google.com/privacy

5. Data security

All network communication used by the integrated SDKs is encrypted in transit via HTTPS/TLS. The App does not handle payment card numbers, passwords, or other sensitive credentials.

6. Children's privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. Because it contains advertising, it is intended for users 13 and older. Parents and guardians should supervise their children's device usage.

7. Your choices

  • Reset your Advertising ID or opt out of personalized ads in your device settings.
  • Uninstall the App to remove all locally stored data.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the App evolves or as laws change. The "Last updated" date above will reflect any changes.
